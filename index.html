Nutzoo - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Introduction

Nutzoo ("the Game") is developed by Kis Daniel. This privacy policy explains how we handle user data.

Data Collection

The Game does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. The Game does not require registration or login.

Advertising

The Game uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's privacy policy: Google Privacy Policy

AdMob may use an advertising identifier (Advertising ID) to show relevant ads.

Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)

Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Data Storage

Game progress (high scores, unlocked skins) is stored locally on the device only and is not transmitted to any server.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
